Tourapist Policy Page

Purpose of the App

Tourapist enables tourists to go on self-guided walking tours, offering an immersive experience with the convenience of exploring at one's own pace.

User-Generated Content Policy

User-generated content is limited to a verified subset of users, known as "tour guides". Tour guides are required to follow our guidelines on walking tour contents to ensure a quality and respectful experience for all users.

Data Collection and Use

We collect users' precise locations to provide the best user experience, enabling tourists to know their exact position for the walking tours. For tour guides, we collect precise locations, pictures taken during the tour, and use their microphone to record tours. This information is essential for creating and offering high-quality, immersive tours.

Data Sharing

Currently, data is not shared with any third parties. Tourist location data is not stored and is only used to enhance the user experience.

User Rights

Users can request to delete their data; however, this action will result in the loss of any purchased tours. Tour guides have full ownership of their tours and may delete a walking tour at their discretion. Deleted tours will no longer be available for purchase and will be completely removed from our platform once all existing purchases have expired.

Content Moderation

Content created by tour guides is moderated through a combination of human oversight and automated processes to ensure compliance with our content guidelines.

Reporting and Complaints

Users can report inappropriate content or policy violations through our website. All reports are handled by our team to ensure prompt and effective resolution.

Policy Updates

Users will be notified via email of any changes to our policies, ensuring they are always informed of how their data is used and protected.
