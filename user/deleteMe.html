How to Delete a User in Our Mobile App

Follow these steps to remove your user account and associated data from our Tourapist:

Step 1: Open the App

Locate the app icon on your device and tap to open it.

Step 2: Ensure You Are Logged In

Make sure you are logged into your account. If not, log in with your credentials.

Step 3: Click on "Delete Account"

On top right, you will see "Delete Account" button next to the Logout button.

Step 4: Confirm User Deletion

A Dialog will open where you confirm deleting your user.

Please note that deleting a user account is irreversible and will result in the loss of all data associated with the account.
