How to Delete a User in Our Mobile App

Follow these steps to remove a user account from our mobile app:

Step 1: Open the App

Locate the app icon on your device and tap to open it.

Step 2: Navigate to Account Settings

Tap on the profile or settings icon usually found at the bottom or top corner of the screen.

Step 3: Select 'Account Management'

Within the settings menu, find and select the 'Account Management' option.

Step 4: Delete User

Scroll down to find the 'Delete Account' option. Read the information carefully. If you wish to proceed, tap 'Delete Account' and follow the on-screen instructions to confirm.

Please note that deleting a user account is irreversible and will result in the loss of all data associated with the account.
